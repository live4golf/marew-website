Our Food

The Art of
Thai Cuisine

“Thai food is not about simplicity. It is about the juggling of disparate elements to create a harmonious finish.”

We have been influenced by Thai and Western food in addition to other Asian cuisines. At Ma Rew you will find dishes that combine and build upon these influences resulting in an exciting menu of authentic and contemporary Thai Cuisine.

The food pictured has all been prepared by our exceptional chefs. The tastes are just wonderful and reflect the subtle combination of sweet, sour, and spice you will find in real Thai food.

Everyone at Ma Rew is passionate about giving our customers an experience of how really good Thai cuisine can be.

Our Story

Ma Rew

The lady in the pictures is my wonderful grandmum, Ma Rew. Ma means mother, and Rew is her name.

At the time of writing she is 90 and has lost count of the number of great grandchildren she has. These photographs were taken at our home in Pak Thong Chai.

Grandmum still prefers to cook in a traditional manner over an open charcoal flame, even though she has a fully fitted kitchen with modern gas burners. Love grandmum.
